With "Clash-Wise-AI", an Autodesk Navisworks plugin, you can go from the uninformative default clash names into descriptive titles that provide immediate context. Our AI-powered intelligence examines each clash data and generates intelligent clash titles that incorporate essential model elements data—including associated levels or locations, object name, type and other relevant metadata and can also determine the associated discipline—ensuring every clash name is informative. You can choose to update the clash title, descriptions, or add as a comment.

Monitor the update progress in realtime or choose to receive an email notification when the update is completed so you can stay informed.
